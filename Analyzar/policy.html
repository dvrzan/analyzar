Tweetalyze Privacy Policy

This policy applies to Tweetalyze iOS app for all information collected or submitted.

Tweetalyze does not require user to register or provide any personal information to operate the app. Tweetalyze does not collect or save any user personal information.

Tweetalyze does not require user to access any user's social network accounts to perform a share action, which could be done by using the iOS system provided share sheet feature.

Tweetalyze will never ask a permission to enable the local push notifications. Tweetalyze will not and cannot send any push notifications to user.

Tweetalyze uses the Twitter website to search for tweets, when user starts a search in Tweetalyze, the app is calling the Twitter standard API, and the search data is not saved in any form.

By using this app, you consent to its privacy policy.

If you have questions regarding this privacy policy, you may email devcontact@icloud.com.

This page will show most up-to-date privacy policy, if any further privacy policy change, the page will be updated to reflect the change.
